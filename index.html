Qty received and CHF value per unit are the only entered figures — every other column is calculated from the rate above. Nothing auto-refreshes: the rate only changes when you click Update Rate, so the number stays put while you fill out the order at the bank and record it in Sage. The rate comes from Yahoo Finance's CHF=X quote, fetched fresh on every click through corsproxy.io (free for requests from a github.io URL or localhost, no signup needed). The same click also refreshes the 5-day hourly trend chart above, cached in this browser the same way the rate is. That third-party relay is best-effort, not a contract — if it's ever down, both the rate and the chart fall back to the last saved version and show a banner. Export CSV stamps the rate and the time it was fetched, for the record.
